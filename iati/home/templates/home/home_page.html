About IATI

IATI works to ensure transparent, good quality data is routinely and systematically used to achieve sustainable development outcomes.

IATI brings together governments, multilateral institutions, private sector and civil society organisations and others to increase the transparency of resources flowing into developing countries.

We encourage all organisations that distribute or spend resources to publish information about their development and humanitarian activities using IATIâ€™s data standard. This is a set of rules and guidance to ensure information is easy to access, understand and use.

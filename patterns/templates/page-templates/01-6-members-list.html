The members' assembly is formed of all members of IATI, and meets in person once a year. Our members fund the initiative, ensuring IATI can continue implementing its work to improve global transparency.

This body has final approval of strategic decisions, including the recommendations on the budget and work plan proposed by IATI's governing board.

Chair and Vice Chairs

The members' assembly is chaired by IATI's Chair and Vice Chairs, who are appointed by the governing board.
